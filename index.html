About us

Who are we?

Macrolab started as a social effort to help and improve the software engineering ecosystem in our hometown. Meetups and hackerspaces were some of the ways we used to help people and teach them about new technologies. Nowadays, we are a company focused on software development, composed by people from many places joining efforts to make the web a better place. As a digital agency, we offer consultancy services for all startup needs, ensuring great products and delightful experiences.

It's all about passion

We are passionate about software and people, that's why we build products and digital strategies that attract people in and encourage them to use your services. We take a different approach than most development companies taking out layers of process that can slow down work, focused on your goals and how to reach them. Our endless curiosity makes us the innovative company you need to turn your idea into a stunning experience.
